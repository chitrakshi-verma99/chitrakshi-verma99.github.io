  • What are three new things you have learned about yourself and your ego as a result of the core learning?
    I have learnt a lot from Core study in the past few weeks. I have been using the idea and practicing Emotional Intelligence more in my life now. Having to manage people in my previous career, i learnt a lot about being aware of my surrounding, about the people and how they feel and how i react with more empathy. I also enjoyed Listening and looping. It was a fun exercise to get to know the rest of the EDA students. The third topic I found really interesting was Neuroplasticity as I've got a science background too, and I love Human Anatomy and Physiology. the idea of our brain being the eternal power to control our habits and daily life, really excites me.
  • What are the role of values, empathy and self awareness in learning and programming?
    Empathy is a way of connecting with other people that shows you understand that they’re experiencing something meaningful—even though you may not understand exactly how it feels for them. In other words, empathy is about finding a way to connect and to be able to say, “I want to understand how this feels to you and let you know that you’re not alone.” Understanding other people's emotions is a key skill in the workplace. It can enable us to resolve conflicts, to build more productive teams, and to improve our relationships with co-workers, clients and customers. Anyone who is dedicated to self-improvement, personal development or the higher goal of spiritual growth needs to actively seek to understand oneself. This is because only when one understands where one lacks, can he or she focus their efforts on what to improve.
